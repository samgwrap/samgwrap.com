About Us

Our Korean-inspired samgyupsal in a wrap is a convenient and portable way to enjoy delicious Korean dish. It typically consists of thinly sliced Samgyupsal, along with various vegetables wrapped in a rice paper.

Each bite of the perfectly cooked meat is guaranteed to be bursting with rich, real flavor. The rice paper binds it all together for a filling dinner on the fly, while the fresh vegetables give a pleasant crunch and counterbalance the savory tones of the meat.
